Brief Info: Child Cab was created due to a very common reason which parents have, so this business is a solution to the problem parents have. Child Cab is a reliable service in which parents don't have to worry about their child after school hours.

Note: This service only runs for schools alone.

About Us

In this business the main aim is to provide a service to the schools which don't have school buses that run. Besides this reason this business takes off a whole lot of stress from parents that don't have the time on their hands to be picking and dropping the children to and from school.
